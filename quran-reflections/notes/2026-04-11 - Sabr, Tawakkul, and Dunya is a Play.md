# Surah Ankabut - Last Day: Sabr, Tawakkul, and Dunya is a Play
**Sat. April 11, 2026 · Molana Farhat Abbas**

---

## Review

1. Why does Allah test us?
2. How He tests us: **fear, hunger, loss of: wealth, life, fruits of labor/life** *(children, job, respect, etc.)*

The answer to getting through these tests: **Sabr** — [[Quran 29-59]]

---

## How to Do Sabr & Tawakkul

### 1. Sabr

- **NOT** passive, **NOT** suppressed emotion — not *"bardash karna"*
- Steadfastness of the soul across **4 fronts**:
  1. Against Anger
  2. In Obedience
  3. Against Sin
  4. During Hardship

> *"Sabr is to faith what the head is to the body."*
> — Nahjul Balagha

Multiple dimensions of success → health problems, spouse problems, school, career, etc. Putting in the work to fix these things *is* success.

---

### Why We Fail at Sabr

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **No Knowledge** | We endure through school because we know it leads to a career. Without understanding why hardship exists and Allah's divine reasoning, we may fail at sabr |
| **No Meaning** | Pain without meaning is unbearable. The soul cannot accept randomness |
| **No Patience** | Leads to rage, despair, abandonment of faith, succumbing to Shaytaan's whisper |

**Root issue is lack of knowledge.** You can go through pain if you understand why hardship occurs — that something better comes out of every trial (*"that's life!"*).

- Sometimes the wisdom is not obvious. That's where you transition to **Tawakkul**.

---

### 4 Steps to Develop Real Patience in Trial

1. **Learn Allah's wisdom through past trials** — study Quran verses, why trials exist
2. **Reflect on past hidden blessings** — what pains ultimately brought good? What delays became doors for growth?
3. **Study the sabr of the prophets** — Yusuf in prison, Ayyub with illness, Zaynab in Karbala
4. **Deepen Marifah of Qadr** — understand that nothing happens without Allah knowing about it; every detail is measured

---

### 2. Tawakkul

**Complete Trust in Allah** → take every legitimate means, then leave the rest to Allah. Accept what comes after trying your best.

- **NOT** dropping all responsibility and doing nothing, waiting for God to save you — *"tie your camel first!"*

---

## Dunya is a Play — [[Quran 29-64]]

- Dunya is not an evil place — just **temporary**

> *"All the world's a stage, and all the men and women merely players."*
> — William Shakespeare

- **Analogy**: We once took Monopoly seriously, but now laugh at kids doing the same. We are living in a big game of Monopoly. Angels look down at us and laugh.
- We need to work hard and care about issues and each other — but **this is not the ultimate reality**.
- **Don't fall into extremes.**

> *Dunya is a play — have sabr. Therefore, build trust with Allah. This is not where the story ends.*

Key verses: [[Quran 29-64]] + [[Quran 29-59]]
