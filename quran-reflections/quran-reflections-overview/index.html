Core Framework

  • Tab'een: Approach every ayah by asking — How is this addressing a problem in my life right now?
  • Goal: Map Surah Ankabut onto your life
  • "Surah" comes from "Sūr" (protective outer wall of a city) — each surah is a protection from a specific danger

Surahs Covered

  • 29 - Surah al-'Ankabut — Primary surah of the series (Sessions 1–5)
  • 114 - Surah an-Nas — Sessions 6–7

Central Themes

  1. Faith is proven through trials
  2. Systems built without Allah are fragile
  3. Believers grow through struggle
  4. Prayer as spiritual discipline
  5. Sabr & Tawakkul as the believer's tools
  6. Surah Nas as the divine shield against waswaas

Key Verses

  • Quran 29:2
    أَحَسِبَ ٱلنَّاسُ أَن یُتۡرَكُوۤا۟ أَن یَقُولُوۤا۟ ءَامَنَّا وَهُمۡ لَا یُفۡتَنُونَ

    Do the people suppose that they will be let off because they say, ‘We have faith,’ and they will not be tested

    — Ali Quli Qara'i
    — "Do the people think that they will be let off because they say, 'We have faith' and they will not be tested?"
  • Quran 29:41
    مَثَلُ ٱلَّذِینَ ٱتَّخَذُوا۟ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ أَوۡلِیَاۤءَ كَمَثَلِ ٱلۡعَنكَبُوتِ ٱتَّخَذَتۡ بَیۡتࣰاۖ وَإِنَّ أَوۡهَنَ ٱلۡبُیُوتِ لَبَیۡتُ ٱلۡعَنكَبُوتِۚ لَوۡ كَانُوا۟ یَعۡلَمُونَ

    The parable of those who take guardians instead of Allah is that of the spider that takes a home, and indeed the frailest of homes is the home of a spider, had they known
